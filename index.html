✨ Meet the Author

Rebecca M

Rebecca is passionate about creating engaging children's literature that combines learning with joy. As a debut author, she believes that every child deserves stories that make them laugh, learn, and dream.

Her inspiration for "One Puppy, Two Puppies, Hey What Do You Do" came from watching children's natural curiosity about animals and numbers. She wanted to create a book that would make counting as fun as playing with puppies!

"Every child has a wild imagination waiting to be nurtured. My goal is to plant seeds of wonder that grow into lifelong love of reading." - Rebecca M
